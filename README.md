# AI Customer Interaction Flows – Automotive Post-Sales

## Overview
This repository explores how AI-driven interaction flows can improve customer experience and operational efficiency in automotive post-sale services.

Includes example interaction flows, prompt design, and success metrics based on real-world operational challenges.

Rather than focusing on complex engineering, the goal is to demonstrate how AI can be applied to real-world operational problems involving customer communication and coordination.

---

## Problem

Postsale operations in industries like automotive services are still highly fragmented:

- Manual scheduling and coordination  
- Multiple communication channels (mainly calls and emails)  
- Lack of structured workflows  
- Poor customer visibility and experience
- No show and loss of customers

This leads to high operational costs and inconsistent service quality.

---

## Solution

Structured, AI-driven interaction flows that manage customer communication across the service lifecycle.

These flows can:

- Automate appointment scheduling  
- Provide proactive updates  
- Reduce manual coordination  
- Improve customer experience  

---

## Example Flow: Appointment Scheduling

1. Customer requests a service appointment  
2. AI agent proposes available time slots  
3. Customer confirms  
4. System sends confirmation and reminders  
5. Follow-up communication before and after service  

---

## Example Flow: Service Follow-Up

- Status updates during service  
- Notification when vehicle is ready  
- Post-service feedback request  

---

## Key Impact Areas

- Reduction in coordination time  
- Fewer manual interactions  
- Lower operational cost  
- Improved customer satisfaction
- Higher confirmation rates
- Lower no show rates

---

## Notes

This is a conceptual and applied repository focused on real-world use cases in post-sale service environments, where customer communication and coordination are still highly manual and fragmented. It is not focused on building production-ready systems.

The intention is to bridge business operations and AI capabilities in customer-facing environments.
